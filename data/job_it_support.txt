Job Title: IT Support Officer

Overview:
We are seeking a reliable and detail-oriented IT Support Officer to provide first-level technical support to internal users. The role involves troubleshooting hardware, software, and network issues, assisting users with IT-related requests, and ensuring smooth day-to-day IT operations.

- Troubleshoot hardware, software, and basic network issues
- Install, configure, and maintain computers, printers, and peripherals
- Assist users with account setup, password resets, and access requests
- Escalate unresolved issues to higher-level support when necessary
- Maintain accurate documentation of incidents and solutions
- Support basic network connectivity and troubleshooting
- Ensure compliance with IT policies and security guidelines
